A simple webapp to help you quickly calculate your current and future grades for a course. Click the New Category button to add a new grading category (tests, quizzes, etc). You can then set its weight and add individual grades to it. Once you've added all your current grades, click the Check Future Scenarios button to see what would happen if you got a certain grade, or what grade you need to obtain a certain average. For example, this is useful for finding out what grade you need on a final exam to obtain a certain grade in the course.
